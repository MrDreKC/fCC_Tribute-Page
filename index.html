Stanley Martin Lieber

Stan Lee a legend in his own right, helping to bring about multiple generations of avid comic book fans and admires alike. From his early beginings in New York being born December 28, 1922, Stanley seem destined to go on to do great things. After being hiring on to work at Timely Comics at 16 barley three years on was he promted to editor-in-chief and later director up until 1972 when he would succeed the owner himself Martin Goodman.

During his time at Timely Comics around 1942 up until 1945 would Stan Lee go on to join the U.S Military until his return from World War II in 1945. All the while he was still dealing with the daily going on's at Timely's, thru receiving letters every Friday from the editors giving him details about what they needed written and by when. Lee would write stories, then send them back on Monday!

The 1960s is where the shift from Timely Comics Stanley Liber would shift into Marvel's Stan Lee. In competition to DC Comics Stan was tasked with coming up with superheros to go against what DC was putting out, which led to him working with artist Jack Kirby to co-create some of the many notable marvel characters we see today such as: The Fantastic Four, The Hulk, Thor, Iron Man, and the X-Men. All of whom lived in a thoroughly shared universe which would lead to the characters coming together to form The Avengers!

Success would follow Stan Lee thorught the rest of his life until the un(timely) day of November 12, 2018, a day when a loving husband and beloved icon would move on from this life onto his next. While he will be forever missed by all who knew of him and all he created, from all of the inspirations that he brought through the creation of many marvel characters and what they represented to many people we say to you Stan Lee. Excelsior!
